About Me

Here's a little bit about myself!

Hello! I'm Angel Chaple, a passionate and self-taught software developer on a journey to build creative and functional web experiences. My background in the IT field has honed my attention to detail, effective communication skills, and ability to solve complex problems, skills that I now apply in my development work. I am currently honing my skills in full-stack development, focusing on front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as back-end tools such as Python and Django. My goal is to leverage my knowledge and skills to create user-friendly web applications that are both aesthetically pleasing and functionally robust.

I’m always striving to improve and expand my knowledge, whether it’s learning new programming languages, tackling challenging projects, or keeping up with the latest web development trends. I am driven by a strong sense of curiosity and the desire to contribute to meaningful, open-source projects. When I’m not coding, you can find me trying out new video games, engaging in cybersecurity challenges, or spending time with my family. I’m excited about the future and looking forward to growing both as a developer and a person.
